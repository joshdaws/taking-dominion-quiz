What this means

AI can absolutely be used poorly. It can flatten voice, muddy arguments, and produce hollow writing. But a thoughtful writer who knows what they want to say can use AI to help articulate it, without sacrificing meaning or intent. In some cases, it strengthens the prose. The ideas are still the writer's. The conviction is still the writer's. AI just helped with the arrangement of words.
